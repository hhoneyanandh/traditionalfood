Meen Kozhungarai (Fish Rice in Leaf)

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Cooked Rice
  • 300g Fish (cleaned, boiled & shredded)
  • 2 tbsp Gingelly oil
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Green chilies (slit)
  • 1 Tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Curry leaves – few
  • Banana / Mandharai leaf for wrapping

Preparation:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, sauté onions, green chilies, and curry leaves till golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, fry until raw smell leaves.
  3. Add tomato and cook till soft.
  4. Add turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt.
  5. Mix in the shredded fish and sauté for 5 minutes.
  6. Add cooked rice and combine gently with the masala.
  7. Place this rice mixture on a banana/mandharai leaf and fold into a parcel.
  8. Steam the parcel for 5–10 minutes so flavors infuse well.
  9. Serve hot — open the leaf and enjoy the authentic Kongu-style Meen Kozhungarai!
